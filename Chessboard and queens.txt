CSES problem: chessboard and queen

Approach

1. simple recursive and backtracking approach
2. 3 1D arrays are used col, diag1, diag2.
    col -> Find if any col is attacked by queen or not
    diag1 -> Find if diagonal with negative slope is attacked by queen or not \
    diag2 -> Find if diagonal with positive slope is attacked by queen or not \
